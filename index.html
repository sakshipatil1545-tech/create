Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about India's first blindfold rebirth program

Is this safe?

Absolutely. Medical clearance is required. Your buddy stays with you throughout. You hold a Stop Card—use it anytime to pause or remove the blindfold. Your wellbeing is our priority.

Medical Safety: Full health screening ensures physical readiness for deep emotional work.

Emotional Safety: Trained facilitators and buddy system provide continuous support.

Do I need experience with this kind of work?

No prior experience is necessary. The program is designed to guide both beginners and those experienced in inner work through a safe, structured process.

What if I can't handle wearing a blindfold?

You're in complete control at all times. You hold a Stop Card that you can use anytime to pause or remove the blindfold. Many participants find the blindfold experience surprisingly comfortable after the initial adjustment.

Will I cry or have emotional releases?

Emotional releases are a natural part of the healing process. The program provides a safe, supportive environment for whatever emotions arise. Many participants experience profound emotional releases that lead to lasting transformation.
